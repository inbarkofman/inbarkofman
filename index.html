About

Hey there 🌔

I'm Inbar, a Senior Product Designer located in London. I'm all about guiding products through their design journey, finding joy in every step of the process.

More than just crafting thoughtful solutions for users, I love understanding their problems. With a B.A. in Human Computer Interaction (HCI) from Reichman University, where I also had the privilege of lecturing, I've spent the past 10 years deeply involved in the dynamic world of big tech, leading projects from concept to completion.

As I look ahead in my career, I'm eager to bring my expertise and steady approach to new challenges. Let's see what lies ahead together — I'm ready for my next journey.
