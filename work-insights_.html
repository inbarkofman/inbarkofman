Hypothesis

The Action Gap

The Challenge: AppsFlyer's rapid growth created a powerful but fragmented ecosystem of siloed tools.

The Friction: What was once streamlined became a manual "hunt" across multiple interfaces.

Increased data density creates an 'Action Gap' — a cognitive burden that turns potential insights into missed opportunities and reactive decisions.

Research Findings

The Efficiency Leak

Our research exposed a critical 'Efficiency Leak': marketers waste 2–4 hours on repetitive manual tasks, with 70% juggling 4+ tabs for a single analysis.

This cognitive friction became the catalyst for our design-led pivot: moving from passive monitoring to a proactive AI partnership.

Problem & Opportunity

Defining the Problem Space

The Problem: As data density grows, the path to execution disappears — creating a critical divide between the platform's power and the user's ability to extract value.

The Opportunity: Minimise the distance between data discovery and business impact. By shifting from a system users 'manage' to one that 'drives' their success, we transform AppsFlyer from a data utility into an indispensable strategic partner.

Beta

Validating the Concept

To validate our AI-led pivot without over-engineering, we executed a high-velocity Lean Beta focused on rapid validation and iteration.

User Flow

I architected a Double Panel UI that decoupled the AI experience from the core platform. This "Lean" architecture allowed us to test the AI logic in an isolated environment with surgical precision, minimising R&D complexity while gathering high-velocity feedback.

Research Methodology

I implemented a mixed-methods framework: pixel-based tracking to map the 'Fragmented Journey' with objective data, complemented by in-depth interviews and usability testing using real-time customer data.

The 'Trust Gap' discovery refocused our roadmap on two pillars: Contextual Evidence (to automate trust) and In-flow Actionability (to close the execution loop).

Design Principles

The Strategic Leap

Moving from an advisor that points at problems to an engine that resolves them. I defined four design principles centred on velocity and psychological safety:

Zero-Distance Action

Eliminating friction between the "Aha!" moment and completion.

Effortless Discovery

Shifting from manual data-hunting to proactive, "pushed" insights.

Trust-Embedded Design

Closing the trust gap by providing immediate visual evidence for every AI insight.

Instant Execution

Moving resolution directly into the existing workflow — the AI doesn't just identify problems, it solves them.

Research & User Testing Sessions

Delivery Model Evaluation

I evaluated four delivery models against their strategic trade-offs:

Delivery Model Concepts — Insights Board, Contextual Layer, Chat, Messenger

1

Chat: Enabled deep reasoning but suffered from "blank canvas" friction.

2

Insights Board: Highly task-oriented but required a high-friction habit shift.

3

Messenger: Felt personal and proactive but detached from data complexity.

4

Contextual Layer: Delivers insights at the optimal time and place, but has limited capacity for global insight management.

Product North Star

The Final Experience

A chat-driven experience became the final concept: a unified space where users can both explore data on demand and receive proactive, AI-powered insights — delivering a seamless blend of push and pull.

Ambient Signaling & Notification Logic

I transformed the global navigation into a proactive 'Pulse' for the platform. Ambient Glow states and a Tiered Badge system create a behavioural bridge between the dashboard and the AI — ensuring revenue-critical alerts remain top-of-mind without creating cognitive noise.

Ambient Glow States & Tiered Badge System

Open Chat: Strategic Briefing

I designed the 'New Chat' state to act as a proactive briefing rather than a static empty state. By instantly summarising recent updates and surfacing high-priority strategic leads, the interface directs focus toward immediate growth opportunities — transforming the AI from a passive tool into an active concierge.

Open Chat — Proactive Briefing State

Conversational Intelligence

I reimagined data interaction by shifting the interface from a static query engine to a collaborative, conversational partner. Instead of just returning raw numbers, the AI provides subjective reasoning and strategic opinions, answering complex questions about spend anomalies and performance trends through natural dialogue.

Conversational Intelligence — Natural Dialogue

High-Severity Urgent States

I established a tiered notification logic that utilises visual 'drama' to command attention during revenue-critical events. When the system detects a high-severity anomaly — a SKAN misconfiguration or major spend leak — the UI shifts to an active, prioritised pop-up state that ensures top-priority issues are confronted before they impact the bottom line.

High-Severity Urgent State — Revenue-Critical Alert

Mission-Critical Trust: In-flow Resolution

For revenue-critical events, I designed a flow that balances urgency with extreme transparency. By allowing users to interrogate the AI's logic and view data results directly within the conversation, we eliminate the need for manual verification. Users can approve complex schema remappings with a single click — breaking the fragmented cycle of cross-platform investigation.

In-Flow Resolution — Evidence & Actionability

AI Thinking State — Transparent Reasoning

Conclusion

Transforming Insights into Strategic Impact

The high-fidelity vision for this AI-driven experience successfully aligned Product and R&D leadership on a next-generation UX strategy.

By shifting from a traditional 'more data' approach to a Design-Led Pivot, we leveraged the 'Trust Gap' concept to clarify the product's unique value over standard dashboards. We established an 'In-flow Actionability' framework, creating a direct link between analytical findings and immediate execution.

100% of research participants identified the offering as a crucial evolution of the platform. This solution became the new North Star for how AppsFlyer users interact with their data.
